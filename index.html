About this project

If you are electronics geek sometimes it is hard to remember the value of the resistor (Just like me :P ). Instead of manually calculating let this website do the heavy lifting for you. Currently it only supports 4 band resistor color 5 & 6 band will be added soon.

Just enter the correct values in the correct field and sit back and have a cup of tea!
